Math Competition with a Computer

How It Works

Do you have what it takes to beat a computer in a math competition? It's not an easy task - only the best of the best of mathemeticians are capable of doing so. It takes both extreme mental haste as well as lightning fast motor-skills to type the correct answer in time. The questions consist of basic arithmetic operations which include addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Your goal is to type the correct answer to the math question as quickly as possible. This is truly an olympiad-level test of one's intelligence. It is the ultimate battle of brain power: human versus machine! Proceed if you dare!
